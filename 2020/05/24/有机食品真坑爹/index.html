You guys shop at Whole Foods, anybody?

Ok, rich white people in here I see.

I’m on HBO and I still can’t afford chicken at Whole Foods.

It’s messed up. Chicken’s 8 dollars a pound at Whole Foods.

8 dollars cause all they’re got is the fancy organic free chicken.

Chickens should not be more than the federal minimum wage.

you shouldn’t be frying hours of nuggets in McDonald’s and only afford half a chicken thigh at Whole Foods, it’s messed up.

Don’t get me wrong. I am all about my animals rights, free range animal. But I still live in a one bedroom apartment with 2 roommates.

I’m not even a free range human being, OK?

I don’t need to eat a chicken that lived better than I did.

The chicken lives 28 acres in Napa, I’m at 200 square foot in Pacoima. That’s messed up, bullshit.

Cause this is how they try to sell you on the chicken. These forester farms and cooperate companies, they are like: the happier the chicken, the better it tastes.

Bullshit, don’t listen to that.

I want to eat the most miserable chicken possible, so that I don’t feel bad of killing a happy chicken.

I want to eat the chicken that’s borderline suicidal you know?

Just lost his custody battle, getting divorced and shit. It’s gonna kill himself anyway, I’m just doing him a favor.

And just to rob you further right, these grocery stores they force you to donate money.

It’s public shaming. That’s what it is.

As you check out all your groceries, the cashier lady is like: Sir, today with your purchase, would you like to also donate a dollar to Save the Children Foundation?

Look bitch, all I got is four packs of ramen noodles. What the fuck do you want from me?

Somebody should give me aa dollar, Ok? I just used coupons and my EBT card.

Somebody behind me in the line was like: Mog, that Aisan chick is really mad.
